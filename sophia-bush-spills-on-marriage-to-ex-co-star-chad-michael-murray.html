Sophia Bush Spills on Marriage to Ex Co-Star Chad Michael Murray

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  215 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Several years ago Sophia Bush and Chad Michael Murray were the hit couple for young TV audiences across America. However, like many celebrity marriages, they didn’t last very long. In fact, Bush and Murray were married a whopping five years before they were divorced – and just like that – the two pretty much went into celebrity oblivion.

Bush, 31,  resurfaced this past week to discuss her five month marriage to Chad Michael Murray, 32,  and she didn’t have many pleasantries to share about her ex- husband:

“My mother once said to me, that if you don’t have anything nice to say, not to say anything at all. At the end of the day, we’re grownups and I actually think what’s interesting about it is when people split in a way that’s unpleasant, they usually just go on, quite literally, hating each other or the person in my situation hates the other person, whatever.”

Around the time of the split there were rumors that Murray cheated on Bush with Paris Hilton while shooting the movie House of Wax.   Since the marriage ended, Bush has kept a low-profile while Murray has had another failed relationship. He was recently engaged to Kenzie Dalton, but the two have since broken up.
