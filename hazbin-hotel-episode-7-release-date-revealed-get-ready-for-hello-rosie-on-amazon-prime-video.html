Hazbin Hotel Episode 7 Release Date Revealed: Get Ready for &quot;hello Rosie&quot; on Amazon Prime Video

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  617 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Fans of Hazbin Hotel can finally mark their calendars for the release of the last two episodes on Amazon Prime Video. Hazbin Hotel Episode 7 is set to debut on Thursday, February 1.

Prime time viewers can catch the episode at 8 p.m. ET/5 p.m. PT, aligning with the previous release schedule of Season 1. The title for Episode 7 has been revealed as “Hello Rosie.”

Notably, Episode 8, serving as the grand finale, will also premiere alongside Episode 7. It shares the release date of February 1 and is titled “The Show Must Go On.” Fans can anticipate the conclusion of the season with these highly-anticipated episodes.

Who Are the Actors in Season 1 of Hazbin Hotel?

Charlie Morningstar is voiced by Erika Henningsen in Hazbin Hotel Episode 5. In the pilot episode, the role was portrayed by Jill Harris, with Elsie Lovelock handling the singing voice.

Stephanie Beatriz lends her voice to Vaggie, the main character. Monica Franco voiced Vaggie in the pilot episode.

Angel Dust, another character, is voiced by Blake Roman in the main series. In the pilot episode and the song “ADDICT,” Michael Kovach provided the voice.

Alastor, a significant character, is voiced by Amir Talai. Edward Bosco voiced Alastor in the pilot episode. Gabriel C. Brown was responsible for the singing voice in the pilot, while Dave Capdevielle handled Alastor’s microphone.

Husk is voiced by Keith David, with Mick Lauer portraying the character in the pilot episode.

Sir Pentious is voiced by Alex Brightman, with Stamper handling the role in the pilot episode.

Niffty’s voice is provided by Kimiko Glenn in the main series, while Michelle Marie voiced the character in the pilot episode.

Vox’s voice is portrayed by Christian Borle.

Valentino is voiced by Joel Perez.

What Will Happen Next in The Hazbin Hotel?

We don’t know what will happen in the next episode of Hazbin Hotel, Episode 7, or if there are any spoilers available. But it’s not too difficult to guess what might happen based on what we’ve seen so far.

In Episode 6, we saw Charlie and Vaggie go to Heaven, where they found out that even Heaven isn’t sure how to redeem someone’s soul. So, it’s likely that Episode 7 will deal with what happens after this revelation. Charlie might feel upset or confused, and the other characters at the Hazbin Hotel will need to help her feel better.

Especially, she’ll need support from Seraphim Emily. Emily is the only angel who understood Charlie’s perspective during their time in Heaven. Depending on what they decide to do, Emily’s support could change things a lot.

This is even more important because Charlie and Vaggie’s relationship might be strained. Now that Vaggie’s secret as an angel is out, things might not be okay between them for a while. It’s unfortunate because Vaggie’s knowledge and skills are crucial for their defense against the next extermination.

Where to Stream Hazbin Hotel Episode 7?

You can see all the exciting episodes of Hazbin Hotel only on Amazon Prime Video.

Summary

Hazbin Hotel is a unique cartoon set in Hell, created by Vivienne Medrano, and debuted its first season after A24’s announcement in 2020. Episode 7, titled “Hello Rosie,” premieres alongside the season finale, “The Show Must Go On,” on February 1, exclusively on Amazon Prime Video. The show features characters voiced by actors like Erika Henningsen, Stephanie Beatriz, and Blake Roman. Episode 7 may address Charlie and Vaggie’s dynamics post-revelation in Heaven. Fans anticipate Emily’s support for Charlie and Vaggie. The series streams exclusively on Amazon Prime Video, adding excitement for its upcoming episodes.
