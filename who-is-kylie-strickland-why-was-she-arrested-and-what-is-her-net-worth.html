When Was Kylie Strickland Arrested?

The social media personality was arrested in June 2022 after a live stream that showed her flashing two minors went viral. From what we gathered, Kylie Strickland got arrested by the Pike County police after the video circulated on several social media platforms, especially on TikTok which also led to the removal of her account on the platform. It was on the 28th of June 2022 that the video was posted online and the police were informed about it the following day, leading to Kylie’s arrest.

People who found the video offensive reported it to the Troup County Sheriff’s Office which then worked with the Pike County Sheriff’s Office to investigate the matter. Confirming this in a Facebook statement, the Pike County Sheriff’s Office related that Kylie Strickland was arrested on a warrant and charged with computer or electronic pornography and child exploitation. Adding that the charge is a felony in Georgia, the sheriff’s office disclosed that Kylie was in jail and awaiting a bond hearing.
