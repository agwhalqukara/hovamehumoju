In a historic moment for the UFC, Dan Ige is stepping in to fight Diego Lopes in the UFC 303 co-main event after Brian Ortega withdrew just hours before the fight due to illness.

The promotion announced the move after UFC insider Ariel Helwani reported that a fight between Lopes and Ige was in the works rather than the scheduled event of Lopes vs. Ortega.

"This is the s--t legends are made of," UFC president Dana White said of the newly-booked fight.

Helwani also noted that Ige did not weigh in yesterday, so the last-minute switch was highly unprecedented.

According to Helwani, Ige's team got word of the attempted switch around 7:30 p.m. ET, about four hours ahead of when he would need to fight.

Shortly after his original report, Helwani said that Ige was in the arena and associated parties said "they feel confident" the fight would happen.
