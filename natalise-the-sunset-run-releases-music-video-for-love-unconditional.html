Natalise + The Sunset Run Releases Music Video for Love Unconditional

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  182 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Natalise (Natalise + The Sunset Run) is the kind of artist who knows how to captivate her listeners without even trying. She doesn’t try to be like all the other pop singers out on the radio today.

What’s unique about listening to her latest single “Love Unconditional” is that there’s virtually nothing on the track but piano and her voice. And rather than having a guitar solo in the song, Natalise introduces a string section.

“Love Unconditional” is a self-affirmation anthem. Because after all, what would we do without self-love? For Natalise, the ocean is a recurring motif in her videos. In her eyes the ocean is a powerful visual metaphor for the emotions we all face in life.

Kenny Choi beautifully captures Natalise by the ocean throughout the video for “Love Unconditional”, where she’s silhouetted by the setting sun over the Pacific and by her piano.
