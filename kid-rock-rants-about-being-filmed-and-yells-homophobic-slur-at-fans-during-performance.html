Kid Rock Rants About Being Filmed And Yells Homophobic Slur At Fans During Performance

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  367 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kid Rock was caught on camera ranting about fans recording his latest performance this past weekend and now, the video of him shouting a homophobic slur at them has gone viral! At the time, the Republican rock star was performing at a Tennessee bar and he obviously did not appreciate the audience having their phones out, filming.

The musician went off while on stage, shouting into his mic: ‘F**k your iPhone, yeah! You f***ing f*****s with your iPhones out!’

And that was not even all! In the same clip that was later shared on social media, he can be seen showing the middle finger to that same person recording before pointing at his crotch and telling them to ‘post this d**k right now.’

According to TMZ, this came after the bar’s security had already asked people not to film during Kid’ Rock’s performance.

This is not the first time that his unfiltered thoughts make headlines.

Previously, Kid Rock was also caught on camera ranting about hating Oprah and Joy Behar, as well as other female celebs during another performance at a local establishment in 2019.

‘I’m just an honest guy who says, ‘Hey, I do not like Oprah Winfrey or Joy Behar.’ F*** Oprah Winfrey. F*** Kathy [Lee] Gifford. I am not the bad guy.’

He went on to slam people who call him ‘f***ing racist.”

Following his unhinged rant, he proceeded to stumble off stage, definitely leaving a lasting impression on those who were there.

Kid also called Joy the B-word before that while on Fox News in 2018.

At the end of the day, however, putting aside his outbursts and the fact that he’s supported Donald Trump in the past, he felt like stressing that everyone should get along regardless of their political beliefs.
