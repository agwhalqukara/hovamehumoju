Hayley Hasselhoff is an iconic name in the fashion industry. She is a plus-sized model and actress from the United States of America.

Today, in this article, we are going to take a deep dive into the personal and professional life of this American model. Further, we will take a look at her journey to success and stardom.

Early Life and Family – Is Modeling in Her Blood?

  • Hayley was born Hayley Amber Hasselhoff on 26 August 1992 in Los Angeles, California, United States of America. She is the daughter of American actor and TV personality David Hasselhoff and Pamela Bach.
  • Hasselhoff has an older sister named Taylor-Ann Hasselhoff. She is also an actress, having worked in multiple movies. Further, Taylor has been married to Madison Fiore since 2023.
  • Hayley grew up in a family full of actors and celebrities. This must have played a crucial role in her current journey in modeling and acting.
  • The American national is in a relationship with Dominic Charles Farrell, a photographer, and actor.
  • The actress is the founder of Teens Helping Teens and a huge supporter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation.

Career Beginning With Baywatch – Acting Credits of the American Model

Hayley Hasselhoff’s journey into the entertainment industry began in 1999 with her father’s TV series Baywatch. She was just seven years old when she portrayed Tammi for two episodes.

Similarly, in 2002, the Los Angeles native appeared in a small role as a little girl with a dog in the 2002 American family film starring her mother, Pamela, and directed by Tom Whitus.

Journey As a Plus-Sized Model – Breaking The Stereotype

Hayley Hasselhoff’s journey as a model began in 2007 when she was just fourteen years old. She signed a contract with Ford Models, a world-famous modeling agency, to begin her career.

More than ten years have passed since the American model’s first modeling assignment. Today, she has established herself as one of the world’s leading curvy models.

Hayley has posed for Torrid, walked in events like the 2014 British Plus Size Fashion Week, and has signed a contract with Wilhelmina Models. Additionally, Pulp Fashion Week in Paris has appointed her as an ambassador.

Similarly, David Hasselhoff’s daughter has been featured in publications and magazines such as InStyle UK, PEOPLE, Seventeen, Runway, and Teen Vogue. Further, she has also graced the television stage by appearing in Good Morning America, the Today Show, and others.

Hasselhoff made history in April 2021 by being the first plus-size model to appear on the cover of Playboy‘s German edition.

Therefore, Hayley Hasselhoff’s journey to stardom broke the myth that you need to be thin as a stick to be sexy and successful. With hard work, determination, and passion, you can surely conquer the world.

Did David Hasselhoff Have Anything to Do With Her Success?

As a father and actor, David must have given her the push she needed to be successful. Hayley began her journey by appearing in her father’s television series, so David does have a hand in her success.

But, the American’s current success in the modeling world is all thanks to her dedication and sheer will. She believes that curvy is sexy. She learned to accept herself from a young age.

Hayley Hasselhoff’s journey as a curvy model is an inspiration to all the plus-sized ladies around the world. With a huge career ahead of her, she is sure to find more fame and success.
