LIL LOADED CLAIMED HE WAS ‘INNOCENT’ IN FRIEND’S SHOOTING DEATH

Khalil Walker, 18, was shot dead in Texas on October 25, and Lil Loaded was arrested in November on a murder charge.

He was indicted on a lesser charge for manslaughter in connection to the incident in March.

The rapper took to Instagram 15 days after the shooting to discuss the shooting, writing: “I appreciate all y’all support.”

“They try to make u look guilty wen u innocent regardless .. we going to get pass this .”

At the time, Lil Loaded’s attorney, Ashkan Mehryari, said his client was innocent, saying: “There’s obviously no malice here.”
